Training Options

OCI offers a complete range of classes for developers moving into objects for the first time or those engaged in advanced distributed systems design. Classes can be delivered at the client site or through open enrollment in our offices in St. Louis. The focus as always is on standards, and the design and implementation of open systems.

OCI has been doing CORBA and OO training for many years. Our instructors are experienced in using CORBA with Java and C++, the use of TAO and JacORB and the implications of migration from older CORBA technologies to later ones. OCI instructors deliver training worldwide.
